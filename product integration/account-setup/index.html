Account Setup

Login to your merchant account on MooPay.

Before we create the product we need to connect with your Wallet.

Connect to Wallet#

Click on Connect Wallet once you are logged into your account.

Connect Options#

Connect your account to a wallet from list of available connect options.

Connected Wallet#

Once connected you will be able to see the following details in your header.

  1. You will see your connected account address
  2. Blockchain info your account is currently connected to.
